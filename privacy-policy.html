📅 Family Alerts — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Family Alerts is a private, personal notification system operated by Junhee Kim. It sends automated SMS messages to family members containing school calendar updates, upcoming event reminders, and daily schedule information.

Who Receives Messages

Messages are only sent to family members who have personally and verbally consented to receive notifications. No messages are sent to unknown or unverified recipients.

What Information Is Collected

This system only stores the phone numbers of consenting family members. No sensitive personal data, financial information, or third-party data is collected or stored.

How Information Is Used

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver family calendar alert messages. They are never sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

Opt-Out

Any recipient can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message or by contacting us directly at jkim12020645@gmail.com.
